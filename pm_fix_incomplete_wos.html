🔧 Incomplete PM Work Orders - Quick Fix

What's Happening:

Your daily PM schedules are generating multiple work orders (one per day). Some older ones were marked as Completed, but their linked pm_instances records still show "Pending". This causes the PM to display as "Pending" on the dashboard.

Example: PM Schedule #24 "pump inspection" shows "Pending" because WO #48 (from 2 days ago) was never marked complete.

Solution: Click "Complete Instance" below to mark these old work orders as done.
