Display Mode:     Table with 13 recent records

Query SQL:
--------
SELECT tc.hj_id AS "Trouble Call", 
       tc.date AS "Date", 
       tc.short_description AS "Summary", 
       IFNULL(mech.lname, "Unassigned") AS "Mechanic",
       IFNULL(op.lname, "Unassigned") AS "Operator"
FROM trouble_calls AS tc
LEFT JOIN mechanics AS mech ON mech.id = tc.mechanic_id
LEFT JOIN mechanics AS op ON op.id = tc.operator_id
ORDER BY tc.date DESC
LIMIT 0, 13
--------

This query retrieves the 13 most recent trouble call records, joining with 
the mechanics table to show assigned mechanics and operators.
